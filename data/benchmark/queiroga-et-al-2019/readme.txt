Instances for the VRP with Backhauls (VRPB) in a TSPLIB-like format.
These instances were generated by "On the exact solution of vehicle routing with backhauls (2019). E. Queiroga, Y. Frota, R. Sadykov, A. Subramanian, E. Uchoa and T. Vidal", submitted to European Journal of Operational Research.
In NODE_COORD_SECTION, the second column defines whether a vertex is the depot (-1), a linehaul (0) or a backhaul (1) customer.
